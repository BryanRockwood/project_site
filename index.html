Welcome to My Front-End Portfolio

Wow - who would've thought. I actually created a full website to use as a portfolio. The whole point of this site is to serve as physical proof that I can create a fully functioning website. It's got bells, it's got whistles - all in pursuit of finding a new job in the front-end world.

Long gone are the days of trying to become a mainframe master. Now is the time for me to move on and join the rest of the world in the modern age of technology. It starts with this very site. This site will take you, viewer, on a journey through my learning journey. Here on this landing page you are witnessing my first (read: first fully finished) website using just simple HTML and CSS.

Hop around the site and check out what I've been working on. While this landing page is a bit boring, I'm hoping you enjoy your stay!
