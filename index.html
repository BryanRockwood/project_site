Welcome to Bryan's Front-End Portfolio

Wow - who would've thought. Bryan actually created a full website to use as a portfolio. The whole point of this site is to serve as physical proof that he can create a fully functioning website. It's got bells, it's got whistles - all in pursuit of finding a new job in the front-end world.

Long gone are the days of trying to become a mainframe master. Now is the time for Bryan to move on and join the rest of the world in the modern age of technology. It starts with this very site. This site will take you, viewer, on a journey through Bryan's learning journey. Here on this landing page you are witnessing his first (read: first fully finished) website using just simple HTML and CSS
